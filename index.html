My Work

Nuclear Thermal Rocket Simulator

The purpose of this project is to construct a digital model of nuclear thermal propulsion rocket engine using numerical tools incorporating fundamental equations to determine whether we can optimize certain components and processes within NP rocket engine that will provide us with maximum efficiency in terms launching cost and also in terms of distance it can travel per kilogram of propellant(i.e., specific impulse).

Automatic potato peeling machine

This machine helps you peel potato in less than 10 seconds. The potato is mounted by pins on a motor-powered stage which rotates about the vertical axis. An arm is attached to a vertical power screw using a torsion spring. A knife is attached at the tip of the arm. As the power screw moves from top to bottom, the knife is pressed firmly against the surface of the potato by the torsion spring and removes its skin.

Alligator egg harvesting robot

The robot aims to harvest alligator eggs in the 10-30cm proximity of alligator nests. The robot will, be controlled remotely by a human operator, and it can withstand operational conditions in a lake and marsh alligator farm area, harvest eggs without provoking the alligator, and comply with other quantitative technical requirements set by the client.
